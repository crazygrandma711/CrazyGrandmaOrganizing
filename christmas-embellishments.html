🎁 Christmas Card Embellishment Checklist

A printable-style checklist of embellishments and supplies for each type of Christmas card.

❤️💚 Traditional Christmas Cards

□ Red & green patterned paper
□ Holly leaves & berries
□ Santa, stockings, ornaments
□ Gold & silver foil accents
□ Classic Christmas sentiments
□ Red/green ribbon & twine
□ Snowflake die cuts
□ Glitter cardstock
□ Traditional stickers & ephemera

⭐ Religious Christmas Cards

□ Nativity die cuts
□ Angel images or stamps
□ Scripture verse stickers
□ Star of Bethlehem embellishments
□ Soft gold & cream cardstock
□ Cross or church silhouettes
□ Pearl or champagne glitter
□ Soft watercolor backgrounds

🍬 Whimsical Christmas Cards

□ Elves, gingerbread, candy canes
□ Bright patterned papers
□ Cute character stickers
□ Fun sentiments (“Jolly”, “Sweet Wishes”)
□ Glitter dots & sequins
□ Playful washi tape
□ Puffy stickers
□ Cartoon-style die cuts

🖤✨ Modern Christmas Cards

□ Black/white/gold geometric shapes
□ Metallic cardstock (gold, silver, rose gold)
□ Thin-line stickers
□ Simple sentiment stamps
□ Modern tree silhouettes
□ Clean star shapes
□ Monochrome patterned paper
□ Vellum overlays

🎅🏼📜 Vintage Christmas Cards

□ Retro Santa images
□ Muted red/green papers
□ Distressed edges
□ Vintage postcards & tags
□ Lace, twine, kraft paper
□ Old-style script sentiments
□ Sepia-toned ephemera
□ Antique-style buttons or brads

🧒🎨 Kids’ Christmas Cards

□ Foam shapes
□ Easy-to-use stickers
□ Bright cardstock
□ Simple stamps
□ Pre-cut shapes
□ Washable glue
□ Googly eyes
□ Glitter foam stars

📸 Photo Christmas Cards

□ Photo frames & mats
□ Border stickers
□ Neutral background paper
□ “Merry Christmas from…” sentiments
□ Double-sided tape
□ Envelopes sized for photos
□ Thin metallic borders
□ Minimal embellishments

🌲❄️ Nature / Winter Cards

□ Pinecones, trees, woodland animals
□ Blue, white, silver papers
□ Snowflake die cuts
□ Winter sky backgrounds
□ Natural twine
□ Soft glitter accents
□ Birch tree silhouettes
□ Frosted acetate overlays

🎁 Miscellaneous / Mixed Style

□ Leftover embellishments
□ Mixed patterned scraps
□ Sentiments that fit any style
□ Neutral cardstock
□ Extra envelopes
□ Random ephemera
