✦ Scrapbooking Quick Tips

  • Sort paper by color family for faster page building
  • Use a "Use First" bin for supplies nearing expiry or overstock
  • Label bins with both category AND brand for quick finds
  • Store stamps upright in binders to save drawer space
  • Keep a small project kit prepped for grab-and-go scrapping
  • Photograph your stash so you can shop your own supplies first
