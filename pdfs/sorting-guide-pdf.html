Sorting Guide

Categories

  • Paper (patterned, solid, scraps)
  • Stickers (sheets, books, loose)
  • Washi Tape
  • Embellishments (die cuts, ephemera, chipboard)
  • Tools (punches, trimmers, adhesives)
  • Photos
  • Seasonal / Holiday Items
  • Projects in Progress

Sorting Steps

  1. Gather: Bring everything to one space.
  2. Group: Sort by category or theme.
  3. Purge: Remove duplicates, damaged items, or things you won’t use.
  4. Store: Place items in bins, folders, or drawers.
  5. Label: Use clear labels so you can find things quickly.
  6. Maintain: Do a quick tidy after each project.

Tips & Tricks

  • Keep a “Use First” bin for items you want to finish.
  • Store by theme (holidays, seasons, kids, travel) or by color.
  • Keep duplicates together so you don’t rebuy.
  • Rotate seasonal items to the front when needed.
  • Use clear containers so you can see what’s inside.
